Eight Layers

Eight-Layer Personas

Expandable details preserve the depth of the old archive while keeping the page easy to scan.

Luo Qingyou | Protecting Dreams Through Order

Her anchors are sage classic Lolita styling, project journals, cameras, tea, old streets, cloud collars, frog buttons, and celadon green.

1. First Impression

Sage Chinese-inspired classic Lolita, scholar-like mood, cloud collar, frog buttons, lace, old camera, journal, tea snacks. Her first impression is gentle reliability.

2. Daily Layer

Arrives early, carries clips, bandages, tape, sticky notes, and scissors. Checks the light before people when taking photos.

3. Behavioral Memory

When Arisu proposes an idea, Qingyou opens her journal before answering. When tense, she adjusts cuffs or camera straps and leaves an 'unexpected' column in plans.

4. Ability Layer

Planning, coordination, aesthetic judgment, archival work, photography, execution, and risk planning.

5. Relationship Layer

Arisu's imagination is the brightest surprise in Qingyou's life. Qingyou protects and prepares, but must learn to leave room for Arisu's dream to breathe.

6. Anxiety Response

Qingyou uses order against anxiety. Her kindness carries fatigue because she often turns care into responsibility.

7. Hidden Conflict

AIGC shakes her value: if tools can plan, organize, and optimize, what remains of the skills she relied on?

8. Growth Layer

She moves from doing every step by hand to choosing what must stay human: judgment, relationships, aesthetics, and shared experience.

Luo Arisu | Searching For Dreams Through Disorder

Her anchors are blue-white JK Lolita styling, keys, rabbits, pocket watches, old libraries, secret doors, daisies, and Alice motifs.

1. First Impression

Blue-white JK Lolita, blouse, pinafore, bow, daisy, key necklace, rabbit charm, pocket-watch bag, and old books. She feels like an Alice figure searching for a rabbit hole at school.

2. Daily Layer

Stops at hallway doors, gives names to charms and objects, and writes diary entries like story openings.

3. Behavioral Memory

Touches her key necklace while thinking and her bow when nervous. She notices doors, mirrors, stairs, and windows longer than other people do.

4. Ability Layer

Concept creation, mood naming, fairy-tale expression, and noticing strange feelings inside ordinary life.

5. Relationship Layer

She depends on Qingyou's execution, but needs her odd ideas to be preserved rather than normalized.

6. Anxiety Response

Arisu uses exploration against anxiety. Detours, daydreaming, and strange sentences delay pressure until she can face it.

7. Hidden Conflict

She fears reality will not match imagination. Perfect generated Wonderlands attract her because they keep dreams beautiful and risk-free.

8. Growth Layer

She moves from waiting for the white rabbit to choosing the door herself, keeping wonder while accepting consequences.

Relationship

Relationship Progression

Turn complementarity, conflict, and repair into scenes that can be written or illustrated.

Progression

Early: light comedic complementarity. Middle: cute habits become pressure. Late: one learns to leave blanks; the other learns to choose.

Interaction Example

Arisu proposes a tea party only lost people can attend. Qingyou answers by giving the dream a date. Their conflict is how to plan getting lost without closing the door.
