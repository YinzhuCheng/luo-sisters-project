Workflow

Web And Asset Pipeline

  • Build the index page and two HTML character sheets first.
  • Crop references from the existing full character sheets into strict character folders.
  • Regenerate textless, borderless, solid-background versions from those crops.
  • Remove the chroma key locally to produce transparent PNG assets and log them in CSV.
  • Replace page placeholders with transparent assets while the web layer keeps final typography and labels.

Story

Story Outline

The sisters prepare a Rabbit Hole Tea Party together.
The surface is a sweet daily-life tea party project. The middle layer is order versus disorder. The deep layer reflects on value, companionship, advice, and generated content in the AIGC era.

  1. Act 1: Leave a Page for the DreamArisu proposes a tea party for people who get lost. Qingyou writes it into her journal.
  2. Act 2: The Dream Becomes FasterClassmates generate posters and plans with AI. Qingyou feels displaced, while Arisu is drawn to perfect Alice imagery.
  3. Act 3: Even Getting Lost Is ScheduledQingyou over-optimizes the invitation and process. Arisu feels her strange sentences have been flattened.
  4. Act 4: Choose the Door AgainBoth admit their fears and reassign AI to repetitive work while they keep direction and relationships.
  5. Finale: An Imperfect Tea PartyRain, lateness, soft-focus photos, and blank journal space make the day truly theirs.
