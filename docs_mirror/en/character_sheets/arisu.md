## Daily Details
- Her bag charms make a small sound when she walks.
- Names keys, rabbit dolls, and music boxes.
- Writes diaries like story openings.
- When anxious, she takes detours and touches her key necklace.
## Growth
She stops waiting for the dream to appear and starts opening the door herself.
